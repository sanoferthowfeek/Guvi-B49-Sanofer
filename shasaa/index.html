Enrollment Process

At SHASAA GROUPS, we have streamlined our enrollment process to ensure a smooth and supportive journey from your first contact with us to your successful career launch. Follow these simple steps to get started

Give a Call & Make an Appointment

Initiating your journey with SHASAA GROUPS is easy. Simply give us a call to speak with one of our friendly representatives. They will assist you in scheduling an appointment at your convenience to discuss your career aspirations and educational needs.

Get Career Counselling

During your appointment, you will receive comprehensive career counselling. Our expert advisors will assess your skills, interests, and career goals to recommend the most suitable training programs for you. This personalized guidance ensures you choose a path that aligns with your aspirations and maximizes your potential.

Enroll in Our Training Program

Once you have chosen the right program, the enrollment process begins. Our team will guide you through the registration steps, including filling out the necessary forms and discussing payment options. With flexible schedules and affordable fees, we make it easy for you to start your training.

Complete Your Curriculum & Assignments

Embark on your learning journey with SHASAA GROUPS. Our structured curriculum is designed to equip you with essential skills and knowledge. Engage in interactive classes, complete assignments, and benefit from the expertise of our top-class faculty. Stay committed, and you'll find yourself advancing rapidly.

Participate in Our Industry-Based Projects

Hands-on experience is a critical part of our training. Participate in industry-based projects that simulate real-world scenarios, allowing you to apply what you've learned in a practical setting. These projects not only enhance your skills but also build a strong portfolio that showcases your capabilities to potential employers.

Resume Building & Interview Mock Training

As you near the completion of your program, our support continues with personalized resume building and interview mock training. Our experts will help you craft a professional resume that highlights your strengths and achievements. Additionally, participate in mock interviews to refine your communication skills and boost your confidence.

Get Certified & Get Assured Interviews

Upon successful completion of your training and projects, you will receive an industry-recognized certification from SHASAA GROUPS. This certification is a testament to your skills and knowledge. With our assured interview opportunities, you will have access to leading employers looking to hire top talent. Step into your career with confidence and readiness.
